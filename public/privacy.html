Privacy Policy for Not Hotdog

Effective Date: June 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Not Hotdog (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles user data.

1. No Data Collection

Not Hotdog does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not track your activity, location, or usage data.

2. No User Accounts

You are not required to register or log in to use the app. No usernames, passwords, or emails are collected.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use analytics tools or third-party tracking libraries. Your use of the app is entirely private and anonymous.

4. Photos

Photos used in the app are processed temporarily in-memory on your device or via secure, transient cloud functions. They are never stored or shared.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed toward children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, we comply with all applicable child privacy regulations.
